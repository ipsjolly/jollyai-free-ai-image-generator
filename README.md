Yes. 5 free generations per day with all 30+ models, no signup required. Pro unlocks unlimited.

No. JollyAI has no content restrictions. All models including NSFW models are available to everyone.

No. JollyAI runs entirely in your browser at [jollyai.online](https://jollyai.online).

**Is there a watermark?**
No. All generated images and videos are watermark-free, even on the free plan.

Yes. You own the images you generate.

It uses AI to expand your simple prompt into a detailed, high-quality prompt with professional photography/art terminology. This dramatically improves the quality of generated images.
